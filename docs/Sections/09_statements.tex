\section*{Reproducibility Statement}

Algorithm~\ref{alg:loss-indexed-confidence} specifies the score construction;
Appendix~\ref{app:theory} gives complete proofs and separates posterior from
numerical error; Sections~\ref{sec:experiments} and
\ref{app:benchmark-protocol} freeze every cohort, action, loss, comparator, and
empty-mask convention.  The supplement includes one-task-per-job scripts,
strict manifest and hash validation, tie-aware AURC analysis, generated tables,
and tests for the estimator, datasets, joins, and renderer.

\section*{Ethics Statement}

The study creates no patient data and uses only released, de-identified
research benchmarks under their source terms.  Dataset composition can encode
device, acquisition, demographic, and annotation biases, so neither confidence
ranking nor abstention should be interpreted as clinical validation.  Selective
segmentation can defer low-confidence cases, but it cannot guarantee safety or
replace domain-expert review.
