\paragraph{Region overlap to worst-case boundary.}
nHD-M32 lowers nHD-risk AURC relative to Dice-M32 in all ten target conditions,
with all ten pointwise intervals excluding zero. Under Dice risk, Dice-M32 is
lower in eight, but only three intervals exclude zero. The strongest separation
is therefore worst-boundary risk, not a symmetric same-name advantage.

\paragraph{Worst-case to robust boundary.}
nHD-M32 is lower than nHD95-M32 in nine target conditions under nHD risk;
nHD95-M32 is lower in eight under nHD95 risk. Only four and two matched-direction
intervals exclude zero. The smaller, condition-dependent gains do not transfer
the nHD Wasserstein corollary to nHD95.

\paragraph{Training-seed sensitivity.}
The prespecified training-sensitivity rule is met only for nHD--nHD95 under
nHD95 risk: five of ten conditions
reverse across seeds and seed 0 disagrees with the majority in three
(Table~\ref{tab:seed-sensitivity-main}). Their displayed values lie in
\([-.0856,+.0236]\); the other three contrast counts are \(1/0/0\).
We call this comparison training-sensitive, not seed-level inference.

\paragraph{Baselines and numerical fidelity.}
A boundary-indexed score has the lowest raw AURC in nine of ten target
conditions under each boundary risk; SDC on FIVES/CLIPSeg-Target is the
exception. SDC and foreground entropy remain competitive for Dice. Dice-M32 and
Dice-Exact have \(\rho\geq.9992\), \(\tau_b\geq.9791\), and at most .363 displayed
Dice-risk AURC difference. For nHD/nHD95, M32--M128 \(\rho\) is
.973--.998/.951--.996 and the largest displayed gaps are .456/.324.

\paragraph{Stress tests.}
In the 360-cell known-posterior study, the correctly specified coupling has zero
posterior discrepancy and M32 mean absolute error .0084--.0087; under
misspecification, mask-law TV is near one while Dice error remains
.0135--.0393. This shows why TV can be too blunt and motivates loss-matched
geometry, not validation of \(Q_p\) on real data. Exact cardinality checks find
zero support for up to 11.5\% of FIVES and 15.4\% of ISIC target references.
Both boundary macro contrasts reverse at \(\gamma=.3\), whereas the Dice--nHD
directions persist. Runtime spans 11.9--4628.8 ms/image for joint three-loss
M2--M32 and 13.2--353.5 for Dice-Exact; outputs are unequal.
